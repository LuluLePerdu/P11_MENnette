## Prefixes
3 letter combo of *[TYPE]*, followed by its *[ACTION]* and then its *[OWNER]*, such as ***btnPlaySnake***


| Combo | Real Type |
| :---: | --- |
|*btn* | QPushButton |
|*sli* | All slider/scrollbar types |
|*sta* | QStackedWidget (Window changer) |
|*prb* | QProgressBar |
|*lab* | QLabel |
|*lcd* | QLCDNumber |
|*pge* | QWidget (page) |
